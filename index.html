|| गुरुदेव ||
Gurudev is an AI based application that can help you with your queries regarding any subjective matter.

It uses GPT-2, an open-source Transformer-based text generator developed by OpenAI that can generate text based on a given prompt. GPT-2 is trained on a large amount of web text and can generate realistic-sounding sentences. It can also be used for tasks such as summarization and question answering.
